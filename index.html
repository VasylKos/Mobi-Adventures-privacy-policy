No Collection of Personal Data
Our application does not collect, store, or process any personal data or information that can be used to identify individual users.

In-App Purchases
All transactions for in-app purchases are processed directly by the native Google Play payment system. We do not store or process any payment information; such data is managed by Google Play in accordance with its security policies.

No Tracking or Analytics
We do not use any tracking or analytics tools to monitor user activity within the app.

Security
Your security is important to us. The application does not require access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive device resources unless it is necessary for the in-app purchase functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page. Please review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@artsyalleycreations.pl.
